# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Food Recipes to be a welcoming project for everyone. Contributors, maintainers, and users are expected to communicate respectfully, collaborate constructively, and focus on improving the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in discussions, reviews, and issues.
- Give and receive feedback with a focus on the code, design, documentation, or user experience.
- Use clear language and assume good intent when resolving confusion.
- Respect differing experience levels and backgrounds.
- Keep project spaces focused on Food Recipes and related work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or repeated hostile comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally disruptive behavior.
- Any conduct that makes the project unsafe or unwelcoming.

## Reporting

If you see or experience unacceptable behavior, report it to the project maintainer through a private GitHub contact method or by opening an issue if private contact is unavailable.

Reports should include:

- What happened.
- Where it happened.
- Who was involved, if known.
- Any supporting links, screenshots, or context.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other action needed to protect the project community. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, consistent, and focused on maintaining a respectful project space.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is inspired by common open source community guidelines and adapted for this project.
